FAJRLY PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

INTRODUCTION

Fajrly ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Fajrly mobile application.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1. Location Data

We collect your device's precise location to calculate accurate Fajr prayer times for your geographic area. Location data is used only for prayer time calculations and is not stored or linked to your user account.

2. Account Information

We collect your email address for authentication and account creation purposes. Your email is used solely for login functionality and account recovery.

3. Prayer Activity Data

We store your wake-up logs and streak data to provide core app functionality. This includes:

  • Daily wake-up records
  • Streak counts (current streak, longest streak, total wake-ups)
  • Math problem completion data

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • Location: To calculate accurate Fajr prayer times using the Aladhan API
  • Email: For authentication and account management
  • Prayer logs: To display your personal progress and motivate consistent prayer habits

We do NOT use your data for:

  • Advertising or marketing
  • Tracking across other apps or websites
  • Selling or sharing with third parties
  • Any purpose other than app functionality

DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services (Google Cloud Platform). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security updates

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Fajrly uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication and data storage
  • Aladhan API: For prayer time calculations (location data sent temporarily, not stored)

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not control their privacy practices.

DATA RETENTION

  • Email: Retained as long as your account exists
  • Prayer logs: Retained as long as your account exists
  • Location: Not stored; used only temporarily for calculations

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Fajrly is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request data correction
  • Opt out of data collection (note: this may limit app functionality)

To exercise these rights, contact us at appfajrly@gmail.com

DATA DELETION

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Email appfajrly@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include your registered email address
  3. We will delete your data within 30 days

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of Fajrly after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

COMPLIANCE

This Privacy Policy complies with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR (for European users) and CCPA (for California users).
